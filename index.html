ICU SETUP AT HOME

Procedure:

  1. We have well qualified critical care team including doctor for free assessment of patient at hospital.
  2. After assessment, we give the care plan to the patient’s family under the guidance of our operation team.
  3. Setting up all the medical equipment at home after the calibration of all medical equipment by qualified and examined hands.
  4. If the patient is on hospital’s ventilator at hospital then we do ventilator testing on our ventilator for 30 to 40 minutes for stabilization of patienton ventilator
  5. After shifting patient at home, our first priority is faster recovery of patient.
  6. All the medical parameters and observation of all medical equipment is to be taken Care by the well qualified Nurse for 24 hours.
